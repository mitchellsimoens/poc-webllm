title: Alachua County Holidays
---
Alachua County holidays are:

- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King, JR Day
- Observed Memorial Day
- Juneteenth
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Veterans Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Thanksgiving Holiday (day after Thanksgiving)
- Christmas Day
- Christmas Holiday (day after Christmas)

Waste pickup may be delayed if it falls on one of these holidays.
